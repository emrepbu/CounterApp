Privacy Policy

Data Collection

Counter App values your privacy. Our app does not collect any personal data.

Data Storage

All counter data is stored locally on your device only. Your data is never sent to our servers or third parties.

Internet Connection

Counter App does not require an internet connection to function. It works completely offline.

Motion & Sensor Data

The app uses your device's accelerometer to enable motion-controlled counting. This sensor data is processed locally in real-time and is never stored or transmitted.

Third-Party Services

Our app does not use any third-party analytics or advertising services.

Data Security

Your data is protected by your device's own security systems. No data is transferred outside the app.

Children's Privacy

Counter App is safe for users of all ages and can be safely used by children as it does not collect any personal information.

Changes to This Policy

Any changes to this privacy policy will be posted on this page.

Contact Us

If you have questions about our privacy policy, please visit our support page.
